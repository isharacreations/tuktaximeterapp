# Privacy Policy

**TukTaxi Meter — Ishara Creations**  
**Effective Date:** March 2026  
**Applicable Region:** Sri Lanka

---

## 1. Introduction

Ishara Creations ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **TukTaxi Meter** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App. By using TukTaxi Meter, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Location Data
- Your device's GPS location is accessed in real time during active trips
- Location is used solely to calculate trip distance, speed, and fare
- Location data is **NOT** stored on our servers
- Location data is **NOT** shared with any third party

### b) Driver Profile Information
- Name, vehicle number, and contact number you provide during setup
- This information is stored locally on your device
- A copy is sent to our secure server for subscription management only

### c) Device Identifier
- A unique device ID (Android ID) is used to manage your subscription
- This ID identifies your device for license activation

### d) Trip Records
- Trip history (distance, fare, date, duration) is stored locally on your device
- Trip records are **never** uploaded to our servers

### e) Subscription Data
- Your license key, activation date, and subscription status are stored on our secure server to validate your premium subscription

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To calculate real-time trip fare using GPS distance
- To verify and manage your premium subscription
- To provide driver profile identification within the App
- To generate trip history and earnings summaries on your device
- We do **NOT** use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
- We do **NOT** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Trip data and driver profile are stored locally using secure Android storage
- Subscription data is stored on our server with reasonable security measures
- We take technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss

---

## 5. Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Location (GPS) | Calculate trip distance, speed, and fare in real time |
| Internet | Subscription validation and fare rate updates |
| Bluetooth | Optional — connect to thermal receipt printer |
| Display Over Other Apps | Show floating fare widget over Google Maps during navigation |
| Foreground Service | Keep GPS active during trips even when screen is off |

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Local trip records are kept until you manually clear them in the App
- Subscription records on our server are retained while your account is active and for up to 12 months after expiry
- You may request deletion of your server-side data through Settings → Delete My Data

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

TukTaxi Meter is intended for use by adult tuk-tuk drivers and is **not directed at children under the age of 18**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be reflected in the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

---

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Ishara Creations through the developer profile on the Google Play Store.
