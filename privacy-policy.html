Privacy Policy

TukTaxi Meter  ·  Ishara Creations

Effective Date: May 2026 Developer: Ishara Creations Region: Sri Lanka

1 Introduction

Ishara Creations ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TukTaxi Meter mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using TukTaxi Meter, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2 Information We Collect

a) Location Data

  • Your device's GPS location is accessed in real time during active trips
  • Location is used solely to calculate trip distance, speed, and fare
  • Location data is NOT stored on our servers
  • Location data is NOT shared with any third party

b) Driver Profile Information

  • Name, vehicle number, and contact number you provide during setup
  • This information is stored locally on your device
  • A copy is sent to our secure server for app registration purposes only

c) Device Identifier

  • A unique device ID (Android ID) is used to register your device
  • This ID is used only to identify your device within our system

d) Trip Records

  • Trip history (distance, fare, date, duration) is stored locally on your device
  • Trip records are never uploaded to our servers

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate real-time trip fare using GPS distance
  • To register your device and provide app access
  • To provide driver profile identification within the App
  • To generate trip history and earnings summaries on your device
We do NOT use your data for advertising or marketing. We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

4 Data Storage & Security

  • Trip data and driver profile are stored locally using secure on-device storage
  • Device registration data is stored on our secure server
  • We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss

5 Permissions Used

  • Location Access — GPS used to calculate distance and speed during trips
  • Internet Access — required for device registration and fare rate updates
  • Bluetooth Access — optional, used for thermal receipt printer
  • Display Over Other Apps — used to show floating fare widget over Google Maps

6 Data Retention

  • Local trip records are kept until you manually clear them in the App
  • Device registration records on our server are retained while the App is in use and for up to 12 months after the device is deregistered
  • You may request deletion of your server-side data by contacting us

7 Children's Privacy

TukTaxi Meter is intended for use by adult tuk-tuk drivers and is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be reflected in the App and on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

9 Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Ishara Creations through the contact details available in the App's developer profile on the Google Play Store.
